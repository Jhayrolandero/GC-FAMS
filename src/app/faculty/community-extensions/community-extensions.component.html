iLoveYouVirus: CodeRED

February 14,2024

What a day! Operation iLoveYouVirus:CodeRED was a major success! Thanks to everyone's effort, the harmless and love-inducing virus spread to a lot of people! Thanks to this virus, infected people got that extra bit of courage to express their heartfelt thoughts to people they hold dear. Love was really in the air! Our digital freedom wall got filled to the brim! Many notes expressing their love not just for their significant others but also to their friends, peers, and those they treat as family were posted. This proves that Valentine's Day is not only a day dedicated to romantic love but also platonic love.
